Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

Aksarben Financial Coaching, LLC ("Company", "we", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how it is protected when you interact with our website, coaching services, and communication channels.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • General financial information voluntarily shared during coaching sessions
  • Information submitted through forms, scheduling tools, or email communication
  • Payment information processed through Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Schedule and conduct coaching sessions
  • Provide financial coaching services
  • Communicate regarding appointments or coaching resources
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Improve our services and client experience

3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed through Stripe, a third-party payment processor. Stripe handles all credit card and payment data securely. Aksarben Financial Coaching does not store or have direct access to full credit card information.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Information may be shared only with trusted service providers necessary to operate the business, including:

  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Email and communication platforms
  • Scheduling or meeting software

These providers are responsible for maintaining their own privacy and security standards.

5. Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect personal information. However, no system can guarantee complete security. By using our services, you acknowledge that electronic communication and internet transmission carry inherent risks.

6. Cookies and Website Tracking

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand website usage and improve functionality. These tools do not collect sensitive financial information.

7. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Review the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of personal information where legally permitted

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party services such as Stripe or scheduling platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external services.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
